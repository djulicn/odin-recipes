Chocolate Lasagna

Easy to make and low in fat! This dessert will be a big hit!

This chocolate lasagna is based on a white chocolate pudding mix and skim milk.

Pieces of a simple milk chocolate can be used as a delicious topping.

Ingredients:

  • packages fat-free instant white chocolate pudding mix
  • 3 cups skim milk
  • 2 tubs fat-free whipped topping, divided
  • 1 box chocolate graham crackers
  • 1 milk chocolate candy bar
  1. Whisk white chocolate pudding and skim milk together in a bowl until thickened and smooth; fold in 1 tub whipped topping.
  2. Line a 9x13-inch casserole dish with 1 layer of graham crackers.
  3. Spread 1/2 the pudding mixture over graham cracker layer.
  4. Repeat layering with graham crackers and remaining pudding mixture, ending with a layer of graham crackers.
  5. Spread 1 tub whipped topping over top graham cracker layer; sprinkle with candy bar pieces.
  6. Chill in refrigerator until set, 2 to 4 hours.
